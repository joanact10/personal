About me

Based in Porto · Padel addicted · Traveler at heart · Big on good company & drinks · FC Porto supporter · Casual plant parent

Hey, I’m Joana! I am 28 years old, and lucky enough to call both Celorico de Basto and Porto home. One’s quiet, the other is vibrant — and somewhere between the two is where I find my balance.
I wrapped up my Master’s in Industrial Engineering and Management back in 2020, and spent the next five years diving into the fast-paced world of analytical consultancy. It was challenging, rewarding, and definitely shaped how I think and work.
In 2025, I took a leap and left the safety net to pursue a long-time passion: web development. I may have traded spreadsheets for code, but the problem-solving drive is the same — and now, it’s paired with creativity, curiosity, and a lot more joy.
